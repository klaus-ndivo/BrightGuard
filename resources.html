Scholarly References (Educator Focus)

These core texts inform the discussion of student development, adversity and educator responses used across this project.

  • Green, M & Piel, JA 2010, Theories of human development: a comparative approach, 2nd edn, Routledge, Oxon.
  • Duchesne, S, McMaugh, A & Mackenzie 2022, Educational psychology for learning and teaching, 7th edn, Cengage Learning Australia, South Melbourne.
  • Margetts, K & Woolfolk, A 2019, Educational psychology, 5th edn, Pearson, Melbourne.
  • McDevitt, TM & Ormrod, JE 2010, Child development and education, 4th edn, Pearson Education, Upper Saddle River, NJ.
  • McInerney, DM 2014, Educational psychology: constructing learning, 6th edn, Pearson Australia, Frenchs Forest, NSW.
