Sexual Harassment, Sexting Pressure, and Bullying

Understanding unwanted behaviors that degrade or intimidate, and how to respond safely.

Sexual harassment includes unwanted comments, messages, images, and behaviours that degrade or intimidate. Sexual bullying uses humiliation or coercion, often amplified online. Education and clear reporting pathways help keep young people safe.
