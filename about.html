The Importance of Awareness

Harmful sexual behavior among young people is a serious issue that requires understanding, compassion, and effective intervention. Early recognition and appropriate response can significantly impact outcomes.

Our resources are designed to help parents, teachers, and community members recognize the signs, understand the underlying factors, and take appropriate action while supporting the wellbeing of all involved.
